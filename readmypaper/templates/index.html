ReadMyPaper

Upload a scientific PDF, let the app strip layout noise locally, and generate a spoken reading in English or Brazilian Portuguese.

First run may download local parsing and voice models. After that, processing stays on your machine.

Cleanup rules

How it works

  1. The PDF is parsed locally with a layout-aware scientific document pipeline (Docling).
  2. Reading order is repaired for multi-column papers using bounding box analysis.
  3. A section whitelist keeps only body content (Abstract, Methods, Results, Discussion, etc.).
  4. A spatial filter drops text fragments overlapping figures and tables.
  5. Scientific notation is expanded for natural listening (e.g. 224×224 → "224 by 224").
  6. Cleaned text is synthesized locally into WAV audio (Piper for speed, Kokoro for quality).
